青玉帶鉤
中文
English
S002

帶鉤是古代男子用來繫腰帶的掛鉤，早在春秋戰國時期就是一種十分普遍的服裝配飾，也是彰顯身份地位的象徵，不僅材質與形制非常多樣，雕刻與裝飾紋樣更是多彩多姿。到了元明清三代，除了實用性的帶鉤之外，出現了大量以把玩觀賞為主的玉質帶鉤，這些帶鉤造型優美、玲瓏奇巧，展現出極為精緻的工藝之美。

這件青玉帶鉤出土於甘肅省徐家坪8號墓，為元朝時期的作品，青玉包涵的色彩層次極為豐富，從白玉中帶有青灰色，到深青灰色中帶有黑斑的種類都有，這件作品呈現青灰色帶有黃褐色斑的質地，一端為龍首裝飾，另一端為浮雕螭紋，螭身呈現彎曲爬行狀，四足凌空，神采奕奕，整體線條顯得自然而流暢，充分表現出玉質的溫潤，是一件高貴典雅的上乘之作。

The belt hook
中文
English
S002

The belt hook is a hook used by ancient men to tie their belts. As early as the Spring and Autumn and Warring States Periods, it was a very common clothing accessory and a symbol of status. Not only are the materials and shapes are very diverse, but also the carvings and decorative patterns are colorful. In the Yuan, Ming and Qing dynasties, in addition to practical belt hooks, a large number of jade belt hooks mainly for play and appreciation appeared. These belt hooks are beautiful in shape, exquisite and ingenious, showing the beauty of extremely exquisite craftsmanship.

This sapphire belt hook was unearthed from Tomb No. 8 in Xujiaping, Gansu Province. It is a work of the Yuan Dynasty. The sapphire contains extremely rich color levels, ranging from bluish-gray in white jade to dark bluish-gray with black spots. This piece of work presents a blue-gray texture with yellow-brown texture. One end is decorated with a dragon head, and the other end is embossed with a chi pattern. The warmth and moistness of jade is a noble and elegant work.
