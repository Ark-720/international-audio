龍泉窯青釉瓷匜
中文
English
S001

龍泉窯為中國南方浙江一帶知名的青瓷產地，以純厚、充滿潤澤的青翠釉色聞名於世，由於地質環境優渥，該地燒製瓷器的歷史相當悠久，加上水運交通發達，從宋朝到明朝的數百年間，龍泉窯青瓷大量外銷亞洲與歐洲各國，為中國的陶瓷史寫下了輝煌的一頁。

龍泉窯的器物種類豐富，宋朝以來受到崇古、復古思想的影響，龍泉窯以古代的青銅器為藍本，燒製了不少仿古青銅器造型的器物。這件龍泉窯青瓷器皿出土於甘肅省汪世顯家族墓，屬於元朝時期的器物，其造型仿自古代盛水的器皿:「匜」，繼承了青銅器的基本形制與端正古雅的基因，但並非完全按照青銅器的原型製作，在規格與紋飾方面，都做了一定程度的改變和創新，將青銅器繁複的線條進行簡化，器形相對較小，線條優美，體現了青瓷簡潔靈秀的風格特徵。

Longquan ware
中文
English
S001

Longquan ware was produced in southern China's Zhejiang province. In particular, Longquan celadon–– pure and lustrous greenware––is famous worldwide. Thanks to excellent geological conditions and well-developed water transport, the area has had a long history of porcelain production. For several hundred years, from the Song dynasty to the Ming dynasty, the Longquan region exported large quantities to Asian and European countries, writing a glorious page in the history of Chinese ceramics.

Many types of ceramics were produced in the Longquan region. In the Song period, kilns began turning out porcelain patterned on the shapes and styles of ancient bronzes. This celadon container was excavated from the tomb of the Wang Shixian clan in China’s Gansu province. Dated to the Yuan period, the vessel is modeled on the classical elegance of ancient yi, a type of bronze water container, but is not an outright copy of the prototype. Changes and innovations are apparent: compared to the bronzeware, the lines are simpler and the scale is somewhat smaller. The artifact’s graceful contours embody celadon’s simple elegance.
